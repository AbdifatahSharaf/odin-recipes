Pan Fried Potatoes

Crispy on the outside and soft inside, perfect as a side dish or light meal.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Paprika or chili (optional)

Steps

  1. Peel and slice potatoes into thin pieces.
  2. Heat oil in a pan.
  3. Add potatoes and spread evenly.
  4. Sprinkle salt and spices.
  5. Fry, turning occasionally, until golden and cooked through.

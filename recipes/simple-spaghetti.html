Simple Spaghetti

A quick, tasty, and beginner-friendly meal made simple ingredients. Perfect for lunch or dinner when you want something filling without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 250 spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil or olive oil
  • 1 medium onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 fresh tomatoes (chopped) or 1 cup canned tomato sauce
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot
  2. Add a pinch of salt
  3. Add spaghetti and cook for 8-10 minutes
  4. Drain and set aside
  5. Heat oil in a pan
  6. Add onions and fry until soft
  7. Add garlic and stir for 30 seconds
  8. Add tomatoes or tomato sauce
  9. Add salt and pepper
  10. Simmer for 5-7 minutes
  11. Add cooked spaghetti into the sauce
  12. Mix well until spaghetti is coated
  13. Cook together for 2-3 minutes

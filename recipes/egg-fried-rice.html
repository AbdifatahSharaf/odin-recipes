Egg Fried Rice

A quick and filling meal made with leftover rice and eggs. Simple, cheap, and ready in minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Add onion and fry until soft.
  3. Crack eggs into the pan and scramble.
  4. Add rice and mix well.
  5. Add salt and pepper, cook for 3-5 minutes.
